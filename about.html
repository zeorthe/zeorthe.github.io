Our History

RTS TECHNOLOGY PHILIPPINES INC.

was incorporated in year 2008 as a system integrator and distributor of Toll Collection System for the Philippine Toll Expressways and Transport industry.

RTSP is a partner of RTS Technology Sdn. Bhd. Malaysia in the Philippines who is at the forefront of providing new TCS technologies and is a major player in the ASEAN region for Toll Collection Systems.

In 2004, RTS Malaysia provided the Toll Collection System for the entire Subic Clark Tarlac Expressway.
